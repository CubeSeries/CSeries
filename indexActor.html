Who?

Mallorie Rodak (born August 16, 1987) is an American voice actress known for her extensive work in English anime dubs. Some of her notable roles include Frieren in *Frieren: Beyond Journey's End*, Yukino in *Fairy Tail*, and the Great Sage/Raphael in *That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime*.

Why We Love Her

Fans are drawn to Mallorie Rodak's exceptional voice acting, where she masterfully embodies a diverse range of characters, such as the thoughtful Frieren in *Frieren: Beyond Journey's End* and the determined Yukino in *Fairy Tail*. Her ability to convey a wide spectrum of emotions and bring depth to her roles deeply connects with audiences. Beyond her talent, her engaging presence within the anime community further endears her to fans.

Natalie Van Sistine

Who?

Natalie Van Sistine (born December 21, 1990) is an American voice actress known for her extensive work in English anime and video game dubs. Some of her notable roles include Yor Forger in *Spy x Family*, Star and Stripe in *My Hero Academia*, and Miorine Rembran in *Mobile Suit Gundam: The Witch from Mercury*. She is also involved in audio engineering and writing.

Why We Love Her

Natalie Van Sistine is beloved by fans for her captivating voice acting, skillfully portraying a wide range of characters with distinct personalities and emotional depth, such as the formidable Yor Forger in *Spy x Family* and the determined Star and Stripe in *My Hero Academia*. Her ability to bring nuance and believability to her roles truly resonates with audiences. Beyond her impressive vocal talent, her engaging presence and connection with the fan community further contribute to her popularity and the affection of her supporters.

Who?

Yoo In-soo is a South Korean actor known for his versatility in portraying diverse characters. From the chilling antagonist Yoon Gwi-nam in "All of Us Are Dead" to the charming Park Dang-gu in "Alchemy of Souls," he demonstrates remarkable range in his performances.

Yoo In-soo's ability to completely transform for each role makes him stand out. He brings authenticity to every character, making him one of the most exciting actors to watch in Korean entertainment today.
